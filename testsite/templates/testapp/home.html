Face Recognition Based Attendance System

Automatic face recognition technologies have made many improvements in the changing world. Smart Attendance using Real-Time Face Recognition is a real-world solution which comes with day to day activities of handling student attendance system.

Face recognition-based attendance system is a process of recognizing the students face for taking attendance by using face biometrics based on high - definition monitor video and other information technology.

All these impressive features

Automated Attendance Tracking

This system uses facial recognition technology to track attendance without the need for any manual intervention. The attendance is recorded automatically in real-time, thus reducing the chances of errors.

Customizable Reports

The system generates detailed reports that can be customized as per the specific needs of the institution. These reports provide valuable insights into student attendance patterns, helping faculty make data-driven decisions.

Time-Saving

With this system, valuable time of both faculty and students is saved as it eliminates the need for taking attendance manually. This means that more time can be devoted to teaching and learning activities.

User-Friendly Interface

Face recognition based attendance systems come with user-friendly interfaces that are easy to use and navigate. They are accessible to everyone, regardless of their tech skills.

Discover Our Process

The face recognition-based attendance system works like magic. During class, the cctv camera captures the image of the whole class 3 times, 1st at 15th minute, 30th minute and 45th minute , which is then send to the server. At server side the faces will be detected and recognized by the machine learning model which predicts the enroll ids of the faces.
